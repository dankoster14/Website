Welcome to DMU Pool & Snooker

We are a Sports Club that is run by the DMU Sport faculty at De Montfort University in Leicester, United Kingdom.

We recently rebranded the club which has only recently become a sports club which allows us to get extra funding from the university, we extended from just playing pool into accomodating the students that prefer snooker by becoming a pool and snooker club.
